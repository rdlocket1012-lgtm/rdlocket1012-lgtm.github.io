# Locket — Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 2026**

Locket ("we", "us", or "our") is a private couples memory app. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it is protected. We have designed Locket so that the minimum amount of data necessary is collected.

---

## 1. Who we are

Locket is an independent app. You can contact us at: **hello@locket.app**

---

## 2. Data we collect and why

### Account
We use **anonymous sign-in** (via Supabase Auth). No email address, name, or social login is required to create an account. You are assigned a random anonymous user ID. If you choose to add a display name or profile photo, those are stored against your account voluntarily.

### Relationship data you create
All content you add — milestones, love letters, map pins, bucket list items, profile details — is stored in your private encrypted database hosted by **Supabase** (a US-based cloud provider). This data is:
- Accessible only to you and your linked partner
- Protected by row-level security policies so no other user can read it
- Never sold, shared with advertisers, or used for any purpose other than showing it back to you inside the app

### Profile photo
If you upload a profile photo, it is stored in a private Supabase storage bucket. It is only used to display your avatar inside the app to you and your partner.

### Location
The map feature stores coordinates (latitude/longitude) that **you** manually drop on the map or enter by place name. The app does not track your location in the background, does not request persistent location permissions, and does not log your movements.

### Device & crash data
We collect basic anonymised crash reports and analytics (device OS version, app version, error logs) solely to fix bugs. This data does not identify you and is never linked to your relationship content.

### Payments
Subscriptions and one-time purchases are processed by **Apple** (via the App Store) and managed by **RevenueCat** (revenuecat.com). We receive only your subscription status (free / premium / lifetime) — never your card details. RevenueCat's privacy policy is at revenuecat.com/privacy.

---

## 3. Artificial intelligence

**Locket does not use AI.** No content you create — photos, letters, milestones, map pins — is sent to any AI or machine learning service. There is no AI processing of your data, now or in any current version of the app.

If AI features are introduced in a future version, you will be shown a new explicit consent screen before any AI processes your data, and you will be free to opt out.

---

## 4. Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers. The only third parties that receive any data are:

| Service | Purpose | Data received |
|---------|---------|--------------|
| Supabase | Database & file storage | Your relationship content (encrypted at rest) |
| RevenueCat | Subscription management | Your anonymous user ID and subscription status |
| Apple | Payment processing | Handled entirely by Apple — we see only subscription status |

---

## 5. Data retention and deletion

- **Deleted items** (milestones, letters, pins, bucket items) are soft-deleted and permanently removed after 30 days.
- **Delete Account** removes all your relationship data, profile, and couple record permanently. Use Settings → Account → Delete my account. We will honour deletion within 30 days.
- You may also email **hello@locket.app** to request deletion of all data associated with your account.

---

## 6. Children

Locket is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

---

## 7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at **hello@locket.app** and we will respond within 30 days.

---

## 8. Security

All data in transit is encrypted with TLS. All data at rest is encrypted by Supabase's underlying infrastructure (AES-256). Access to your couple's data is enforced at the database level — no other user can read your records even if they know your IDs.

---

## 9. Changes to this policy

We will post any material changes to this page and update the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
